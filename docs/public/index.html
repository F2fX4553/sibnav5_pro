Welcome to Sibna


The Sibna Protocol is a **production-ready**, **secure**, and **resilient** communication ecosystem.


Why Sibna?

Traditional messaging apps lock your data in their silos. Sibna empowers you to build your own private communication networks with military-grade security.


Key Features

  • **End-to-End Encryption**: Built on the Double Ratchet.
  • **Offline-First**: Reliable messaging even on spotty networks.
  • **Cross-Platform**: SDKs for Python, Web (JS), and Mobile (Flutter).
